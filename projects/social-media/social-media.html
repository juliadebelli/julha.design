Description

Historically, I have been developing pieces for social media even before it became a business. Back in 2012, I used to illustrate news for TibiaBR.com.

In 2015, as social media was taking it first steps as its own field, I was at the forefront of this new way of doing publicity, using state-of-the-art tools, creativity and design methods to deliver work that mattered. All the while being completely clueless most of the time, for it was a very young and experimental field.

Then social media began having its own rules and methods, and I grew along with it once again.
